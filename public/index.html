The LUMINA Method

Four layers.
One coherent output.

01
Client Intake & Scope
Before any work begins, LUMINA captures the client's systems, rates their data maturity on a 25-point scale, documents scope boundaries, and logs known data problems — in their own words. The client acknowledges it. No surprises.
02
Live Engagement Workspace
Every session adds to the living record. Notes, decisions, blockers (typed as Data/People/Technical/Unclear), sub-stages, and deliverables — all timestamped. The document builds itself as you work.
03
Weekly Pulse Signal
Every Friday, 10 minutes. Log overall health (green/amber/red), what was delivered, active blockers, and an honest analyst note. This builds a timeline of how the engagement actually went — invaluable for retros and next proposals.
04
Leave-Behind PDF Report
Started at the midpoint, finalised at close. One click generates a branded PDF with cover page, executive summary, systems map, process docs, decisions log, maturity score delta, and open recommendations. The client keeps it forever.
